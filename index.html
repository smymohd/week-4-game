Get It To The Total!

Directions
You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four images below. By clicking on the image. you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by making your total score to random number you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each image is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game start the game will change the values of each image.
